Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AJA Trading LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with its internal QuickBooks Online integration application ("the App"). This App is used solely for internal bookkeeping and accounting purposes by AJA Trading LLC and is not offered to the general public.

1. Information We Access

The App connects to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API to access financial data including accounts, journal entries, invoices, bills, and related accounting records belonging to AJA Trading LLC. This information is accessed solely for internal bookkeeping, tax preparation, and financial management purposes.

2. How We Use Information

All data accessed through the App is used exclusively for:

  • Internal financial record-keeping and bookkeeping for AJA Trading LLC
  • Tax preparation and compliance
  • Reconciliation of trading accounts and financial statements

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose any QuickBooks data to third parties. All data accessed through the App remains confidential and is used only by AJA Trading LLC's authorized personnel.

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth credentials (refresh tokens) used to authenticate with QuickBooks Online are stored locally on AJA Trading LLC's private systems and are not transmitted to or stored on any third-party servers. We use industry-standard security practices to protect these credentials.

5. Data Retention

Financial records accessed through the App are retained in accordance with applicable tax and accounting laws and regulations. OAuth tokens are refreshed as needed and revoked when no longer required.

6. Your Rights

As this App is used solely for internal purposes by AJA Trading LLC, there are no external users. If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at the address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
